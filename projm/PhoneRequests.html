Eligibility


Once Eligibility for an individual has been determined certain criteria will assist in the assignment of stipend amounts:


If an individual requires the use of a cellular device to make/receive calls only and they are not able to get their own cellular plan then they would only be eligible for the feature phone.


If an individual requires the use of a cellular device to make/receive calls they would only be eligible for the $35.00 stipend.  (Talk plan)


If an individual requires the use of a cellular device to make/receive calls, send/receive texts, and/or uses the device to access ResCare email and other Data Services for work purposes then they would be eligible for $75.00 stipend.  


This would be an individual that would spend most of time away from a “home” office and need Data to have business continuity.  (Data Plan)
